struct visioncpp::internal::LocalOutput< false, IsRoot, LC, LR, RDCN< OP, RHSExpr, Cols, Rows, LeafType, LVL > >

LocalOutput specialisation for reduction neighbour operation(RDCN).

It creates the local accessor to store the output of the operation which is going to be used as an output for its parent.
